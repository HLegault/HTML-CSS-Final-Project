We are a bakery that loves to feed your sweet tooth craving! Established in 2016 in St. John's Newfoundland we have grown to 10 locations across Canada!

Our mission is to provide people everywhere with treats everyday to satisfy that sweet tooth! By having cakes, cookies, brownies, a daily speciality pastry and of course cupcakes! We hope to have something for everyone!

Our headoffice is located in our St. John's location!
